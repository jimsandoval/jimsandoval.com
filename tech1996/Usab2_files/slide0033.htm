Tognazzini’s Observations:
lGeneral population is heavily skewed towards Sensories (80%)
l“Engineers and psychologists are among the most highly educated individuals ... and have the greatest resistance to intuitive cognition” (Berne, 1958)
lSoftware teams are skewed towards Introverts and Intuitives (twice western-world averages)
lFor effective user-interface design:
–Extroverts are natural communicators
–Intuitives are superb pattern-recognizers and highly imaginative
–Thinking vs Feeling is relatively unimportant
–Perception vs Judgement is relatively unimportant
