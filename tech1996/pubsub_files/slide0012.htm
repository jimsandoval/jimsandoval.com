l“Publish and Subscribe” is a technology developed by market data vendors such as Reuters for efficiently broadcasting large amounts of data to a large user base. These networks are largely one-way: data out to the user. l“Publish and Subscribe” is gaining currency as an effective way to model business-events in lower volume real-time groupware applications. lWhat is presented here is a specific design strategy for  groupware. Implementations used by market data and database vendors are somewhat different.
