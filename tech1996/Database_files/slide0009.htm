Data Warehousing
lEnable strategic business analysis (”Data Mining”) without interfering with OLTP systems
lData is usually aggregated into sets (rolled-up)
lThree components:
–Population - Getting data in
–Administration - Making it accessable, trustworthy
–Decision Support - Getting information out
lHigh demands for volume, complexity and ease-of-use vs immature, expensive technology
lDriving factor for tradeoffs is ROI for strategic business analysis
